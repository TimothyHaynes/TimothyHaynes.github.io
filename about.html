Why I want to be a developer?

I have always wanted to work with technology. I never really saw myself as a coder, but I always wondered. It wasn't really until this year when I started taking courses online that I found that I actually enjoyed creating something from scratch. I like the fact that I can use developing as a way to get in touch with my creative side. I honestly never thought I was creative until I started taking these courses. So, I started Grand Circus to get into the field and see where developing takes me.
